Features

Dashboard

Fully Customizable and modular dashboard to fit your industry and businessneed.

Application

Access the dashboard anywhere; on your SecuriPass mobile device application or on the web.

Notifications

Send SMS or Push notifications to selected visitors at any time during their visit to your facility

Device

A simple to use Android based mobile device to help you capture your visitor information. No need to write or type...just swipe and automatically capture visitor information

A paperless solution, eliminate the need for manually writing names on the logbook and storing them in a room that exposes them to security breach, risk or water or fire damage.

Automatically and accurately capture any information you need - name, ID number, age, gender, checkin and checkout and purpose of visit - by a simple swipe of the ID or Passport.

Visitor data is safely and securely stored on the cloud for access to the facility owner who can view, analyze and generate reports about their visitors.
